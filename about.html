About Lunar Beauty

Lunar Beauty is a cosmetics line for men, women, and everyone in between. Our website includes Lunar Beauty’s enchanting palettes and Manny’s fun and informative how-to videos on all things cosmetics. Manny MUA gave up the path to medical school to start his makeup career. Manny is now one of the most influential YouTube and Instagram stars and is Maybelline’s first male ambassador. His love for makeup and outgoing personality led Manny to create Lunar Beauty.
